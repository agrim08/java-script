There are 7 primitive data types in JS:
1. numbers:- all int and decimals type.
2. strings:- used for texts.
3. boolean:- used for taking decisions (true-false).
4. Undefined:- Value taken by a variable that is not yet defined ('empty value').
5. null:- also means empty value.
6. symbol:- unique value and cannot be changed.
7. bigInt:- larger ints.
